Biography

Born in London, I grew up reading reference books over fiction. I always loved the way that science and technology could teach us about the world we really lived in, and was inspired by an idea of the future where the cosmos was within reach. This brought me towards astronomy as a career, but when I eventually reached university I was to be diappointed. Steadily increasing debt and being in a degree that, while fascinating, gave few practicle skills weren't what I wanted from life. Though I'm still motivated by cosmology, I switched my focus to computer science and engineering. There is a much greater satisfaction in creating than learning on its own. My hope then, as it is now, is to make a living out of being creative with technology; maybe some day I'll use the proceeds to see the universe for myself...
